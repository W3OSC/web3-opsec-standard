## Individual Account Settings

- Account Settings:
    - Security & sign-in > 2-Step Verification
        - [ ]  Enable any, but passkeys or authenticator recommended
        - [ ]  Remove any App passwords
    - [ ]  Security & sign-in > Skip passwords when possible > **Enabled**
        - *Skipping password entry allows you to renew logins in public places without having to type out your password or store it in your clipboard*
    - [ ]  Security & sign-in > Recovery email > **Enabled**
    - [ ]  Security & sign-in > Recovery phone > **Disabled**
    - [ ]  Third-party Connections > Third-party apps with account access > Remove all unnecessary
    - [ ]  Security & sign-in > Recent security activity > Log out from all unnecessary devices
    - [ ]  Security > Enhanced Safe Browsing > **Enabled**
