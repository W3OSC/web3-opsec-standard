# Domain 5: Incident Readiness & Response

### **Incident Response Planning**

**SP-IR-001: Comprehensive Incident Response Plans**
- Organizations MUST maintain documented incident response plans for all critical systems
- Plans MUST include specific procedures for Web3-related incidents
- Response procedures MUST define roles, responsibilities, and escalation paths
- Plans MUST be reviewed and updated at least annually

**SP-IR-002: Web3-Specific Response Procedures**
- Incident response plans MUST include multi-sig wallet compromise scenarios
- Smart contract exploit response procedures MUST be documented and tested
- Emergency contact procedures MUST include specialized Web3 incident response resources
- Asset recovery procedures MUST be documented for different attack scenarios

**SP-IR-003: Regular Plan Testing and Exercises**
- Incident response plans MUST be tested through tabletop exercises and simulations
- Web3-specific scenarios MUST be included in testing exercises
- Exercise results MUST be used to improve response procedures
- Testing MUST be conducted at least quarterly

### **Detection and Monitoring**

**SP-IR-004: 24/7 Security Monitoring**
- Organizations MUST implement 24/7 security monitoring for critical systems
- Monitoring systems MUST detect anomalous activities and potential security incidents
- Alerts MUST be delivered through multiple redundant channels
- Monitoring data MUST be retained for forensic analysis

**SP-IR-005: Web3-Specific Monitoring**
- All wallet addresses MUST be monitored for unauthorized activity
- Smart contract interactions MUST be monitored for suspicious patterns
- DeFi protocol integrations MUST be monitored for exploit attempts
- On-chain analytics MUST be integrated into monitoring systems

**SP-IR-006: Threat Intelligence Integration**
- Organizations MUST consume relevant threat intelligence feeds
- Web3-specific threat intelligence MUST be integrated into monitoring systems
- Threat intelligence MUST be used to update detection rules and procedures
- Intelligence MUST be shared with relevant stakeholders and communities

### **Incident Classification and Response**

**SP-IR-007: Incident Classification Framework**
- Organizations MUST define incident severity levels with clear criteria
- Incidents MUST be classified within defined timeframes
- Response procedures MUST vary based on incident severity
- Classification decisions MUST be documented and auditable

**SP-IR-008: Emergency Response Capabilities**
- Multi-signature freeze/pause capabilities MUST be available for critical contracts
- Emergency communication channels MUST be pre-established and tested
- Break-glass procedures MUST be available for critical situations
- Emergency contacts MUST include specialized Web3 incident response resources

### **Communication and Coordination**

**SP-IR-009: Secure Incident Communication**
- Organizations MUST establish secure communication channels for incident response
- End-to-end encrypted channels MUST be used for sensitive incident communications
- Communication procedures MUST be pre-defined for different stakeholder groups
- Communication channels MUST remain functional during incidents

**SP-IR-010: Stakeholder Notification Procedures**
- Stakeholder notification procedures MUST be documented and practiced
- Public communications MUST be coordinated and approved through designated channels
- Communication templates MUST be prepared for different incident types and audiences
- Post-incident communication reviews MUST be conducted

### **Forensics and Evidence Preservation**

**SP-IR-011: Digital Forensic Capabilities**
- Organizations MUST have access to digital forensic capabilities
- Evidence preservation procedures MUST be established and followed
- Chain of custody MUST be maintained for all forensic evidence
- Forensic analysis MUST be conducted by qualified personnel

**SP-IR-012: Web3 Forensic Capabilities**
- On-chain forensic capabilities MUST be available for blockchain analysis
- Transaction tracing and analysis tools MUST be accessible
- Relationships with blockchain forensic specialists MUST be established
- Legal requirements for blockchain evidence MUST be understood

### **Security Training and Awareness**

**SP-IR-013: Comprehensive Security Training Program**
- Organizations MUST provide regular security awareness training to all personnel
- Training MUST cover Web3-specific threats and attack vectors
- Training effectiveness MUST be measured and tracked
- Specialized training MUST be provided for high-risk roles

**SP-IR-014: Phishing Simulation and Testing**
- Organizations MUST conduct regular phishing simulation exercises
- Simulations MUST include Web3-specific phishing scenarios
- Results MUST be tracked and analyzed for improvement opportunities
- Additional training MUST be provided to personnel who fail simulations

**SP-IR-015: Social Engineering Prevention**
- Organizations MUST provide social engineering awareness training
- Verification procedures MUST be established for sensitive requests
- Personnel MUST be trained to recognize common social engineering tactics
- Reporting mechanisms MUST be available for suspicious contacts

### **Business Continuity and Recovery**

**SP-IR-016: Business Continuity Planning**
- Organizations MUST maintain business continuity plans for security incidents
- Critical systems MUST have backup and recovery capabilities
- Recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO) MUST be defined
- Business continuity plans MUST be tested regularly

**SP-IR-017: Asset Recovery Procedures**
- Asset recovery procedures MUST be documented and tested
- Coordination with exchanges and other platforms MUST be established
- Legal procedures for asset recovery MUST be understood and documented
- Insurance coverage for digital assets MUST be evaluated and maintained

### **Continuous Improvement**

**SP-IR-018: Post-Incident Analysis**
- Organizations MUST conduct post-incident reviews for all significant incidents
- Root cause analysis MUST be performed to identify underlying issues
- Lessons learned MUST be documented and shared appropriately
- Security controls and procedures MUST be updated based on incident findings

**SP-IR-019: Metrics and Reporting**
- Incident response metrics MUST be tracked and analyzed
- Regular reports MUST be provided to leadership and stakeholders
- Trend analysis MUST be performed to identify improvement opportunities
- Benchmarking against industry standards MUST be conducted

**SP-IR-020: External Coordination**
- Relationships with law enforcement and regulatory bodies MUST be established
- Information sharing with industry peers MUST be implemented
- Participation in threat intelligence communities MUST be maintained
- Coordination with Web3 security organizations MUST be established
